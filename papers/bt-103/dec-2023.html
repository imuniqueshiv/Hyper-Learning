Previous Year Questions

Q.1. a) Answer the following as per direction given in brackets:
  • i) Nishkarsh is playing football. (Change into passive Voice)
  • ii) He said, "His horse died in the night." (Change into Indirect speech)
  • iii) She said, "Oh no! I missed the train." (Change into Indirect speech)
  • iv) They were doing good work. (Change into passive Voice)
  • v) Sam has written a letter. (Change into passive Voice)
  • vi) He said, "I have lost my mobile phone." (Change into indirect speech)
  • vii) "Why haven’t you phoned me?" he asked me. (Change into Indirect speech)
b) Choose the correct form of the verb that agrees with the subject.
  • i) Mathematics (is, are) John’s favorite subject, while Civics (is, are) Andrea’s favorite subject.
  • ii) Eight dollars (is, are) the price of a movie these days.
  • iii) (Is, Are) the tweezers in this drawer.
  • iv) Your pants (is, are) at the cleaner’s.
  • v) There (was, were) fifteen candies in that bag. Now there (is, are) only one left!
  • vi) The committee (debates, debate) these questions carefully.
  • vii) Either my shoes or your coat (is, are) always on the floor.
